Crafting Connection Through Culture and Content

María del Carmen Gutiérrez blends creativity with strategy in everything she does. Raised in the sun-drenched village of Benimarfull, she grew up surrounded by orange groves, sea breezes, and stories shared over long lunches — a rhythm of life that still shapes her approach to work and connection.

After studying Marketing and Communication in Valencia, María del Carmen launched her career helping small local brands find their voice online. Her curiosity and passion for culture led her to explore other corners of Europe — from Lisbon to Florence — eventually specializing in digital branding and cross-cultural storytelling.

She's now a fully remote professional, working with global clients while staying rooted in her love for the Mediterranean lifestyle. Whether she's crafting a social media campaign, building a content strategy, or sipping coffee on a sunny terrace, María del Carmen believes in authentic connection, clear messaging, and the power of beautiful, meaningful stories.

When she's not working, you'll likely find her:

  • Planning her next seaside escape
  • Experimenting with bold flavors in the kitchen
  • Wandering local markets with a camera in hand

She lives for sunshine, good food, and stories that connect people — and she brings that spirit into every project she touches.
